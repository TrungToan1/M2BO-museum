what can you do?

in the national video game museum you can experience the timeline of games. as you go through the museum you will slowly move from the oldest games to the newer games. this is a wonderful experience. in this game musuem you will not only play games but also learn the history of how games we know today came about. our musuem is for young and old so everyone is welcome.

History

the story begins in 2008 in a garage box in the city of the hague. through 2 friends hansan tsdemr and pascal rappailes. the garage box was soon too small, so they looked for a new place and ended up in a shed. the shed had no heating and everything had to be transferred in the winter. when this location also had to be cleared out. became the collection of 10 arcade cabinets at that time transferred to an old showroom of classic cars. there they could not work on the cabinets due to lack of space. so the search continued for a suitable space. in 2011 they had found a good place. only due to the growth, the hobby approach was no longer appropriate and. they decided to take a professional approach. in 2015 she switched to a company called playworks BV. in 2016 they had the idea to turn it into a museum. At the beginning of 2017, a location was found in collaboration with the municipality. the formal v&d building in the city center of Zoetermeer. on December 1, 2017, the museum was ready for visitors.
